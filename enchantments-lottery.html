The Enchantments Lottery - March 2025

Problem

What are the odds to win the Enchantments lottery? How can we maximize our chances to gain admission to the Enchantments? What factors are important to keep in mind? Our project aims to analyze the odds of winning the Enchantments lottery in order to maximize our chances. We explore the data in Tableau to analyze and understand the different factors that will help us maximize our chances.

Data Overview

We have two sets of data: the 2024 Enchantment lottery statistics, and the historical weather data for the area:

Important Notes

Some important things to note about the application process:

  • Lottery applications open on 2/15/2025 on recreation.gov
  • NWT can submit one application that includes three options: a 1st preference, a 2nd preference, and a 3rd preference
  • Each preference allows them to specify the following choices:
    • The preferred entry date
    • The area (zone) of the Enchantments to visit
    • The number of people in the party

    Analysis

    To analyze our odds, we created a Tableau story that provies some basic lottery statistics, what the odds are for different zones/group sizes, how to truly maximize our chances, and a final application recommendation.
